### Top 3 Recommendations

2. **Brief (Offer):** "I've packaged everything I know about Message Threading into a comprehensive system" sets up an offer that never lands. State exactly what the reader gets (audit? course? done-for-you service?), what it costs or how to qualify, and why they should act now. Without a concrete offer, the strategic clarity of the rest of the ad has nowhere to convert.
3. **Copy (Closing):** Delete "We look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience" entirely. This passive, first-person-plural sign-off contradicts the confident, direct first-person voice used throughout the rest of the ad ("I've audited over 340 DTC landing pages"). If a sign-off is needed, match the tone: "I'll show you exactly where your Friction Stack is costing you sales."
