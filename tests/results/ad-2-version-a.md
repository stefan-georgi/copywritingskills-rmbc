### Top 3 Recommendations

1. **Mechanism (5/25):** Replace the empty "AI-driven features streamline your workflow" with a named, specific mechanism — e.g., "TaskFlow's Dependency Engine maps every task's upstream blockers, so when one team finishes, the next team's work auto-queues in priority order." A real cause-and-effect chain ("A triggers B which produces C") is the single biggest lever to differentiate from every other PM tool running the same feature-list ad.

2. **Research (9/25):** Replace "helps teams get more done in less time" with a specific ICP pain grounded in their language — e.g., "Your ops team is burning 11 hours/week copying status updates between Slack, Sheets, and Jira" (sourced from customer interviews or support tickets). The job titles in the testimonials (Operations Manager, CTO) hint at the real audience; the body copy should speak to them directly instead of addressing generic "teams."

3. **Brief (12/25):** Collapse the competing angles into one dominant theme and thread it from hook to CTA. Currently the ad tries to be about time-saving AND AI AND all-in-one AND scale AND security. Pick one — if the mechanism is about eliminating manual handoffs, make the entire ad about that. Then add urgency to the offer: "Start your free 14-day trial — first 200 signups get the Pro tier free for 60 days" beats "Start your free trial now" which has zero reason to act today.
